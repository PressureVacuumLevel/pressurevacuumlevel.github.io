Eco-Friendly Sustainable Stripping Tools
Not just a  GREEN  look


Together with a group of experts, WEICON TOOLS Green Line has been developed. A tool range of up to 97 % renewable raw materials is recyclable and has an excellent life cycle assessment.


Like the classic line in blue, the Green Line tools, developed in cooperation with experts, include various cable knives, wire strippers, cable strippers and multi-purpose strippers. In total, the Green Line features fourteen different tools.


In terms of quality, they are in no way inferior to the classic line and are just as robust and durable.


The material used:


► is based on plant oils, natural waxes, fibres and minerals


► goes through a closed CO2 cycle and is recyclable


► is primarily produced without the use of the finite resource crude oil


► has polyamide-like characteristics


► is produced CO2 neutrally and with renewable energy
Production with energy generated in-house
Resource-saving
From plastic to cardboard

The tools are produced almost entirely with energy generated in-house – thanks to photovoltaic systems.

Faulty tools and the sprue produced during injection moulding are remelted at the beginning of the production chain.

The tools come in recyclable cardboard packaging.
